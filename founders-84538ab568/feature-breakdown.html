What we might be missing

Feature gap analysis — the current set above benchmarked against the live dashboard, the PRD (incl. the agentic parent layer + child-safety), and competitors (Beanstack, Scholastic, Renaissance / Accelerated Reader, Epic!). Grouped by theme; priority reflects launch impact, not effort.

Learning depth & integrity

Missing capabilityWhy it mattersPriority
Writing capture & promptsOur own hypothesis says the writing block is the lever for comprehension and written-expression scores — yet there's no writing feature in the set.P0
Comprehension checks / quizzesVerifies real reading (not just minutes) and is table-stakes against Accelerated Reader; feeds the parent projection.P1
Reading-level placement (Lexile / RIT)Needed to recommend right-level books and to ground the fall-score projection in a real baseline.P1
Audiobook / read-aloud loggingInclusive of striving and younger readers; most summer programs already count listening.P2
Anti-gaming / log integrityA competition invites inflated logs — needs reasonable-pace limits plus parent/teacher confirmation.P1

The agentic parent layer

Missing capabilityWhy it mattersPriority
Weekly analysis digestThe core agentic differentiator — turns raw logs into "what happened and what to do." Specced in the PRD but not yet in the feature set.P0
Fall-score projection modelThe parent's hook; currently a concept on the dashboard, needs to be a real modeled, explainable feature.P1
Recommended nudges (confirm-before-action)Closes the loop from insight to action and writes back to the kid app with parent approval.P1
