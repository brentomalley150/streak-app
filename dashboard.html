💡What the Research Says

Volume matters. Kids who read 1,000+ minutes over summer don't lose ground. Kids who read 1,500+ actually grow.

Writing makes reading stick. Writing a sentence using a detail from what was read is what specifically moves comprehension and written expression scores — don't skip the writing block.

Consistency beats marathons. 5 days of 15 min beats 1 day of 75 min. The streak isn't just for points — it's the actual mechanism.
