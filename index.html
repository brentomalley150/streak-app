How it works

Three things every day. That's the whole challenge. Tap to check off, earn points, climb the ranks.

1 📖

Read

15 minutes a day. Any book, comic, magazine, recipe — even the back of a cereal box counts.

+1 point
2 ✏️

Write

At least 50 words. Doesn't have to be perfect. Stuck? Tap "💡 Need ideas?" — there are 67+ ready to go.

+1 point
3 🎯

Wrap-Up

5 minutes to lock it in. Talk about what you read, learn a new word, fix one sentence in your writing.

+1 point
Bonus: Do all 3 the same day → that's a Perfect Day. +1 extra point. 🌟
